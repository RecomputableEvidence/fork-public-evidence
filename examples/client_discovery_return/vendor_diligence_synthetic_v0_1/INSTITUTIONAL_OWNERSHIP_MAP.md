## Response ownership

If Fork preserves evidence that something failed, is missing, is not checked, is stale, is out of scope, or is source unavailable, the Vendor Risk Lead owns business response, with escalation to the Chief Risk Officer and Security Governance Lead when security-sensitive artifacts are involved.

## Escalation path

Vendor Risk Lead to Chief Risk Officer for business risk. Security Governance Lead for security-sensitive evidence. Commercial Legal Counsel for legal concerns. Compliance Director for compliance concerns.
