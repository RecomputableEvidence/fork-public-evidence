# Scenario 09 Non-Claims Panel

Fork can record that a validity-changing event was recorded in one system, another system had no confirmed visibility of that event, a downstream actor relied on stale or partial state, and synchronization, consumption, or revalidation remained required.

Fork does not establish global visibility, global consumption, current authority, current validity, current approval, current policy satisfaction, current evidence sufficiency, current regulatory compliance, current legal sufficiency, current execution eligibility, current external acceptance, negligence, excuse, or correctness.

Fork does not approve, certify, score, authorize, determine compliance, determine admissibility, establish legal sufficiency, decide acceptance, or judge correctness.
