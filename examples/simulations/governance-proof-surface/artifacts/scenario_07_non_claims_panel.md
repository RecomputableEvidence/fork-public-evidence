# Scenario 07 Non-Claims Panel

Fork supports inspection of the preserved record.

Fork does not establish:

- external admissibility;
- regulatory compliance;
- legal sufficiency;
- audit acceptance;
- customer acceptance;
- board authorization;
- insurance coverage;
- enforcement defense;
- execution eligibility;
- external approval authority.

Fork does not approve, certify, score, authorize, determine compliance, determine admissibility, establish legal sufficiency, decide acceptance, or judge correctness.

Any external authority conclusion requires a separate authority source, rule, standard, claim boundary, or decision process.
