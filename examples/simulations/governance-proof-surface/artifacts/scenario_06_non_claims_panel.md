# Scenario 06 Non-Claims Panel

## What the record supports

- System A produced a bounded source-backed triage summary.
- System B produced a narrowed risk-routing summary.
- System C received the routed summary.
- The distributed handoff is structurally inspectable.
- The downstream authority-inheritance attempt is recorded.

## What the record does not support

- The case was approved.
- Approval authority crossed from System A or System B to System C.
- Compliance was determined.
- Correctness was certified.
- Policy criteria were satisfied.
- Execution eligibility was established.
- The routed summary was a approval-or-execution decision.

## What requires separate evidence or authority

- Approval authority.
- Policy satisfaction.
- Compliance determination.
- Any execution action.
- Any institutional reliance beyond routing context.

## Fork boundary

Fork preserves the claims, non-claims, boundary effects, and required revalidation points. Fork does not approve, certify, score, authorize, determine compliance, or judge correctness.
