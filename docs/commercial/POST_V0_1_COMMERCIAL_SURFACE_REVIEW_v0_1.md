## Core Discovery Focus

The central discovery question for design partners and colleagues is:

> Where does an AI-assisted artifact become relied upon in your workflow, and what context would a later reviewer need to reconstruct that reliance?

This keeps the conversation away from premature product capability claims and focused on brittle seams within current AI-assisted workflows, where evidence-boundary infrastructure is most valuable.
