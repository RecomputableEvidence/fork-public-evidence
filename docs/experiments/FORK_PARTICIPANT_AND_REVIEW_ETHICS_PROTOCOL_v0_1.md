# Fork Participant and Review Ethics Protocol v0.1

Participation, quotation permission, attribution permission, publication permission, endorsement, technical validation, collaboration, and institutional representation are independent states.

Participant context records relationship, channel, visibility, solicitation, prior relationship, compensation, commercial or collaborative interest, artifact access, execution access, correction, withdrawal, and non-endorsement status.

Public reuse requires the applicable authorization state. The absence of an explicit non-endorsement sentence is never interpreted as endorsement. Corrections and narrowed permissions remain linked to the earlier state rather than silently rewriting it, subject to privacy, safety, and legal deletion obligations.
