# Fork Interaction Provenance Protocol v0.1

Every experimental observation must preserve how it was elicited and what the observer could access.

Interaction provenance records who selected the question, explanatory turns, artifacts supplied and accessed, experimenter corrections, participant corrections, response mode, spontaneity, inclusion rationale, exclusions, non-response state, channel limitations, and retrieval provenance tier.

The protocol surfaces experimenter influence and selection effects without deciding that influence invalidates the observation. Invited non-respondents and declined participation must remain countable when an outreach corpus is summarized.
