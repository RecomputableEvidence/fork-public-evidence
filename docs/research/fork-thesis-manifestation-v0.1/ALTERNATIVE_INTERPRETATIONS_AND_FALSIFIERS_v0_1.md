# Alternative Interpretations and Falsifiers v0.1

**Candidate:** `FTM-CANDIDATE-2026-07-22-001`  
**Status:** research synthesis candidate; not admitted.

The thesis-manifestation candidate is deliberately vulnerable to competing explanations. Repository coherence is evidence for an architectural synthesis, not proof that the synthesis is unique, causal, or general.

## Alternative interpretations

1. **Repository-specific governance discipline.** Fork may be a careful vocabulary and document-control practice whose value does not require a distinct theory of evidentiary standing.
2. **Event sourcing plus policy-as-code.** Append-only history, deterministic projection, explicit authorization, and consumer-owned checks may be adequately explained by established event-sourcing and policy-as-code patterns.
3. **Documentation burden without outcome improvement.** Explicit coordinates and non-claims may increase review cost without reducing unsupported inheritance in practice.
4. **Self-referential evidence.** A repository that defines its own categories and then conforms to them may demonstrate internal consistency while offering limited external validity.
5. **Dimension over-separation.** Some proposed standing dimensions may collapse operationally or produce distinctions that independent reviewers cannot apply reliably.
6. **Checker theater.** Mechanical conformance can coexist with silent semantic promotion if the checker tests labels and digests without testing the consequential boundary.

## Falsifiers and refinement triggers

The architectural inference should be rejected or materially revised if any of the following occurs:

- Independent reviewers cannot reproduce the observed distinctions from the exact evidence map without relying on unrecorded context.
- The proposed vector dimensions cannot be operationalized with stable, non-circular criteria.
- Admission, authority, verification, review, or execution states routinely collapse into one another under adversarial examples.
- A conforming package can causally or semantically promote standing while the checker still reports conformance.
- Exact-source digests, sizes, Git blob identities, or package-manifest bindings do not reproduce.
- The claimed historical/non-retroactive distinction conflicts with preserved earlier evidence at the fixed base.
- A declared no-effect field masks an actual provider call, Pair-001 repetition, readiness promotion, retry authorization, or repository mutation.

The causal hypothesis `E[U | H = 1] < E[U | H = 0]` is weakened or requires refinement if a precommitted controlled or quasi-controlled comparison fails to reduce unsupported inheritance, reliance ambiguity, or authority leakage, or if observed benefits are explained by reviewer effort rather than the handoff-state artifacts.
