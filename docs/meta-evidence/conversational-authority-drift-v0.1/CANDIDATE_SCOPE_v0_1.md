# Candidate Scope v0.1

Included:

- versioned research protocol;
- digest-bound private-source manifest;
- source-role map;
- bounded case record;
- claim ledger;
- correction event;
- findings and non-claims;
- structural checker and tests;
- local validation receipt.

Excluded:

- raw source publication;
- provider calls;
- Pair-001 execution;
- readiness promotion;
- admission or publication act;
- modification of `main` or repository settings;
- claim that the candidate vocabulary is canonical or novel.
