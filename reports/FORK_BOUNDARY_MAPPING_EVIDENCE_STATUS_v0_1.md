## Evidence posture

This packet does not ask a reader to accept Fork as a category by assertion.

It records concrete boundary evidence showing where:

- a bounded source claim exists,
- evidence persists,
- a boundary is crossed,
- a downstream assumption appears,
- authority is attempted to be inherited,
- Fork preserves evidence,
- Fork does not inherit authority,
- non-claims and unresolved questions remain visible.
