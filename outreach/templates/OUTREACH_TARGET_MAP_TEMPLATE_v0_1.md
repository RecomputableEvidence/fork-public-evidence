Do not commit populated target lists to the public repository.

## Review cadence

Review after every 5 sends.

Track:

- sent;
- responses;
- curiosity;
- meetings;
- wrong-person referrals;
- hard no;
- no response;
- message changes needed.

## Stop rule

Stop first-wave outreach when either:

- 2 serious conversations are booked, or
- 12 first-wave targets have been contacted.
