The review request is narrow. I am not asking for endorsement, certification, legal opinion, or validation of any compliance claim.

The useful review questions are:

- Does the evidence boundary hold?
- Can the verification path be reproduced from public materials?
- Is the non-claim boundary explicit enough?
- Is there wording or structure that could cause a reader to infer claims Fork does not make?
